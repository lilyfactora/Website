About me.

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, California and am now in my third-year at Reed College in Portland, Oregon. At Reed, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in mathematics with a minor in computer science. After obtaining my bachelor's degree I plan on obtaining a Master of Science in a related field or beginning my career in the tech industry.

As an aspiring software engineer or developer, I am fascinated by the dynamic challenges and collaborative nature of the tech industry. My academic journey at Reed, focused on mathematics and computer science coursework, has equipped me with a strong foundation in problem-solving and analytical thinking. Serving as a research assistant, I've honed these skills by leveraging technology to unravel complex concepts. This experience not only solidified my passion for programming but also emphasized the importance of teamwork in achieving innovative solutions. Looking forward, my goal is to build an abundant and versatile toolkit so that I may contribute meaningfully to projects that excite me, push the boundaries of technology, and positively impact the world.
